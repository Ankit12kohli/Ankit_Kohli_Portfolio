Hi. I'm Ankit Kohli.

And this is My Portfolio, a simple way, to showcase my skills and achievements.

I am a tech savvy and incredibly passionate about content development and information architecture. I feel excited to learn new technologies and find new avenues to contribute towards a great CX.

I started my journey as an Aircraft Maintenance Engineer after completing my degree and holding DGCA certified BAEMEC for Heavy Aircraft(HA), Light Aircraft(LA) and Piston Engine(PE). It's been almost 12 years wherein I have explored various roles such as a trainee engineer, design engineer, senior technical writer, Information developer and Assistant Manager of CS & Sales Operations. Now, as a Manager of Product Knowledge Enablement, I love mentoring my team including hardware and software documentation and video creators. I try my best to ensure I optimally leverage the strengths of my team.
